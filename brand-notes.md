# Phortivia Brand Notes

**Name:** Phortivia  
**Pronunciation:** for-TIV-ee-ah  
**Domain:** phortivia.com  
**Origin:** Greek *phortion* (“cargo/load”) + Latin *via* (“road”)

## Positioning

A modern, high-touch freight brokerage that reduces uncertainty through proactive updates, disciplined carrier selection, and personal accountability.

## Primary line

**Freight, carried with clarity.**

## Alternative lines

- A better road for every load.
- Capacity with accountability.
- The clear way forward for freight.
- Built for cargo. Made for the road.

## Visual system

- Deep navy: `#07161F`
- Signal lime: `#D8FF63`
- Route cyan: `#67E8F9`
- Warm off-white: `#F4F4EE`

The identity avoids generic red/blue trucking visuals. The lime route line communicates motion, visibility, and forward progress.
